Welcome to my personal homepage

I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong. My PhD supervisor is Prof. Jacky Keung. For details on my PhD thesis, please click.

I obtained my MEng degree in Software Engineering from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, where I was supervised by Prof. Han Bo. My teaching experience includes positions as (1) an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB), (2) a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Software Engineering at Daffodil International University, Bangladesh, (3) a lab facilitator in the Department of Computer Science at the City University of Hong Kong, and (4) developing graduate-level courses at Mälardalen University, Sweden.

I have previously crafted innovative methods and remedies for

  • Concept drift issues in software defects for the development of reliable models,
  • Class imbalance issues in software defect prediction, and
  • Oversampling techniques to alleviate class imbalance issues.
I also enhance the state-of-the-art ML methods.

In addition, I actively engage with research communities both locally and internationally, serving on organizing and technical committees, acting as a technical co-chair, and reviewing for high-impact peer-reviewed journals.

My mission is to contribute to the development of high-quality, secure software systems for societal benefit. If you share the same value, please reach out for collaborations.
