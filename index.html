About

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. Proir to that, I was a lecturer at the Department of Software Enginering, Daffodil International University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. My PhD supervisor is Dr. Jacky Keung. I also work very closely with Dr. Kwabena Ebo Bennin.

I got my M.Eng. degree from International School of Software, Wuhan University, China. I was very fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Han Bo.

My current research focuses on data science for empirical software engineering. Recently, I am trying to employ the knowledge of data stream mining, statistical modeling, and empirical investigation to produce more reliable defect prediction models.
